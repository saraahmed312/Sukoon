Our Story

Emotional Support Duaas was born out of a shared experience among the developers - the realization that in times of emotional distress, many Muslims turn to duaas (supplications) for comfort, but often struggle to find the most relevant ones for their specific situation.

As practicing Muslims and software developers, we saw an opportunity to bridge technology and spirituality by creating a platform that helps users find appropriate duaas based on their emotional state or needs.

Our mission is to make the wealth of Islamic spiritual resources more accessible and personally relevant to Muslims around the world, especially those going through difficult times.

Our Sources

We take great care to ensure all duaas on our platform are authentic and properly sourced from recognized Islamic texts. Our primary references include:

  • Quran: The holy book of Islam, containing many supplications within its verses.
  • Sahih al-Bukhari and Sahih Muslim: Authentic collections of Prophet Muhammad's (PBUH) sayings and actions.
  • Hisnul Muslim (Fortress of the Muslim): A well-known compilation of authentic duaas from the Quran and Sunnah.
  • Riyad as-Salihin: A collection of hadiths on various aspects of Islamic life.

We also consult with Islamic scholars to verify the authenticity and appropriate contexts for each duaa in our database.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every Muslim can easily access spiritual support tailored to their emotional needs. In the future, we hope to:

  • Expand our database to include more specialized duaas for specific life situations
  • Add audio recordings of duaas for those who prefer to listen
  • Develop mobile applications for easier access
  • Provide resources in multiple languages to serve a global audience

We welcome feedback and suggestions from our users to help us improve and better serve the Muslim community.
